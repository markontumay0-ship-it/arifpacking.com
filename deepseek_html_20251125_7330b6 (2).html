Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Arif Packing was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to transform ideas into memorable packaging that tells your brand's story. We believe that exceptional packaging is not just about protection—it's about creating an experience.

We focus intensely on design, ensuring every sticker, paper pack, foldable card, cardboard box, and flexible pouch reflects professionalism and creativity. While we partner with trusted local printers for production, the design, precision, and brand-focused approach come entirely from our dedicated team.

Our Mission

To deliver visually appealing, brand-focused packaging designs that elevate products and create memorable customer experiences. We combine creativity with strategic thinking to develop packaging solutions that drive brand recognition and customer loyalty.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's most trusted packaging design partner, recognized for innovation, quality craftsmanship, and exceptional client satisfaction. We aim to set new standards in packaging design while supporting local businesses in their growth journeys.

Our Services

Comprehensive packaging solutions for businesses of all sizes

Packaging Solutions

We offer a complete range of packaging services to meet your business needs

Printing & Design

We bring your ideas to life with precision and creativity. From logo placement to full packaging layouts, our design team ensures every print reflects your brand identity. We specialize in offset, digital, and flexible packaging printing, with options for embossing, foil stamping, and UV coating for a premium look.

Flexible Packaging Solutions

Protect and present your products in style. We offer a wide range of flexible packaging, including pouches, sachets, and custom bags suitable for food, cosmetics, and retail. Our materials are durable, safe, and designed to enhance product shelf appeal.

Custom Packaging & Folding Boxes

Tailored packaging that fits your product perfectly. We produce foldable boxes, rigid boxes, and specialty packaging with custom sizes, shapes, and finishes. Ideal for gifts, cosmetics, and high-end products.

Stickers & Labels

High-quality stickers and labels to elevate your brand visibility. We offer durable, full-color, and specialty finishes that are water-resistant, scratch-proof, and eye-catching. Perfect for retail, promotions, and product identification.

Fast Delivery & Reliability

Your deadlines matter. Our streamlined production and logistics ensure fast turnaround without compromising quality. We manage everything from design to delivery, so you can focus on your business.

Consultation & Brand Support

We don't just produce packaging—we help brands succeed. Our experts advise on material choice, design trends, and packaging efficiency to maximize both aesthetics and functionality.

Small & Large Quantity Orders

Whether you need 500 units or 50,000, we can handle it. Our flexible production system caters to both startups and large businesses, keeping costs efficient and quality consistent.
